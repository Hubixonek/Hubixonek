## About me

I am a hybrid of a Junior Frontend Developer and UX/UI Designer, passionate about creating 
digital products that are both functional and user-friendly.

With experience in e-commerce, I have developed a strong understanding of user behavior 
and the importance of translating customer feedback into meaningful UX improvements 
that support business goals.

Currently finishing my Computer Science engineering studies, I continuously improve my 
frontend development and design skills. I enjoy working across the entire product process - 
from user research and interface design in Figma to implementing responsive applications 
with React and TypeScript.

Besides my GitHub projects, I showcase my UX/UI work through a dedicated portfolio 
created in Webflow CMS.
